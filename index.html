⚡ DSM-IV → DSM-5 Key Changes (PNLE Context)

Hypochondriasis→Illness Anxiety DisorderSomatic preoccupation WITHOUT significant sx; or Somatic Symptom Disorder WITH distressing sx
Somatization Disorder→Somatic Symptom DisorderFocus on thoughts/feelings/behaviors, not symptom count
Pain Disorder→Somatic Symptom Disorder (pain)Eliminated as separate category
Mental Retardation→Intellectual DisabilityIQ alone insufficient; adaptive functioning also required
Asperger's Disorder→ASD Level 1Now under Autism Spectrum Disorder umbrella
Dysthymia→Persistent Depressive DisorderIncludes chronic MDD + dysthymia
OCD (Anxiety chapter)→OCD & Related (own chapter)BDD, Hoarding, Trichotillomania, Excoriation added
PTSD (Anxiety chapter)→Trauma & Stressor chapterNew dissociative subtype; Criterion A revised
Gender Identity Disorder→Gender DysphoriaDestigmatized; focus on distress not identity itself
Dementia/Delirium chapter→Neurocognitive DisordersMajor & Mild NCD; Alzheimer's, Vascular, LBD, FTD subtypes
Bereavement Exclusion (MDD)→Removed in DSM-5MDD can be diagnosed during bereavement
Substance Abuse + Dependence→Substance Use DisorderSingle spectrum; mild/moderate/severe based on criteria count
